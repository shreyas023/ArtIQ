About this course

Throughout this course, you'll master the art of tackling diverse projects, including intricate endeavors like explainer videos. By the course's conclusion, you'll be adept at generating creative concepts and executing them flawlessly to narrate compelling stories.

We'll kick off by delving into the intricacies of comprehending a client's script or crafting one yourself, leveraging AI if necessary. Then, I'll guide you through the process of ideating visually striking elements, drawing inspiration, and conducting research to enhance your project. Additionally, you'll learn how to meticulously plan your workflow using storyboards.

Subsequently, we'll transition to creating scenes using Illustrator, followed by crafting impeccable voiceovers with AI assistance, simplifying the animation initiation process. Once the groundwork is laid, we'll dive into After Effects to breathe life into your project. Expect invaluable insights on synchronizing animations with voiceovers and seamlessly transitioning between scenes.

Furthermore, you'll discover techniques for incorporating captions, utilizing AI or implementing them manually within After Effects. Lastly, we'll add finesse to your animation through fundamental effects and sound enhancements, culminating in a polished final product.
